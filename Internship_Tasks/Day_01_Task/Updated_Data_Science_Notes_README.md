## 1Ô∏è‚É£ What is Data Science?

-   **Definition:** The study of data to find meaningful insights that
    help a business grow.
-   **Simple Words:** Being a "Digital Detective" who uses math to solve
    problems.
-   **Real-Life Example:** Amazon looking at your past purchases to show
    you "Recommended for You" items.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2Ô∏è‚É£ Types of Data Analytics (Solving the Business Problem)

When a business has a problem (BP), they use these four steps:

### 1. Descriptive (What happened?)

-   **Definition:** Looking at past data to summarize history.
-   **Example:** A coffee shop seeing that they sold 50 lattes
    yesterday.

### 2. Diagnostic (Why did it happen?)

-   **Definition:** Digging deeper to find the cause of a result.
-   **Example:** Realizing latte sales were high because it was a very
    cold day.

### 3. Predictive (What will happen?)

-   **Definition:** Using patterns to guess future trends.
-   **Example:** Guessing that if next Tuesday is cold, they will sell
    50+ lattes again.

### 4. Prescriptive (What to do now?)

-   **Definition:** Suggesting the best action to take.
-   **Example:** Deciding to buy extra milk on Monday so they don't run
    out on Tuesday.

## 3Ô∏è‚É£ What is Data?

-   **Definition:** Information consisting of facts, numbers, or
    measurements.
-   **Primary Source:** Data you collect yourself (Internal).
    -   Example: Asking your customers to fill out a feedback form.
-   **Secondary Source:** Data from someone else (External).
    -   Example: Looking up a company's stock price on Yahoo Finance.

## 6Ô∏è‚É£ Roles in DS / AI / ML

-   Data Analyst: Creates reports and charts.
-   Data Scientist: Builds predictive models.
-   Data Engineer: Builds the systems to store and move data.
-   ML Engineer: Puts machine learning models into real apps.
-   NLP Engineer: Teaches computers to understand human speech/text.
-   Business Analyst: Connects data insights to business goals.
-   BI Developer: Builds dashboards for managers to watch.
-   Analytic Engineer: Cleans data specifically for analysts to use.
-   Data Architect: Designs the big-picture "map" of the data system.
-   AI Engineer: Builds smart systems like chatbots.
-   AI Specialist: Expert in a specific AI field (like vision or
    ethics).
-   Product Analyst: Analyzes how people use a specific app feature.
